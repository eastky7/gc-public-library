Frequently Asked Questions

Library cards are available for free to any resident of Ghre County. Residents ages 17 or older can recieve a library card by presenting a photo ID at any Ghre County Public Library. Residents below age 17 must have an accompany parent or guardian. Library cards are available to non-residents for a one time fee of $5.00.

Ghre County Public Library recognizes a need for assistive technologies and provides them per request. Special accomodations can be requested by telephone, in-person, or via email during normal operating hours.

To report a problem or issue with our website, please fill out this form and submit it to helpdesk@gclibraries.org

Submitted issues are addressed in sequential order. You will be notified using your given contact information when the issue is resolved.
